## Recording Tips

1. **Screen Recording on iPhone:**
   - Swipe down from top-right corner
   - Tap screen record button (circle)
   - 3 second countdown
   - Tap red status bar to stop

2. **Best Practices:**
   - Turn on Do Not Disturb (no notifications)
   - Full battery or plugged in
   - Good lighting if showing physical phone
   - Go slow - easier to speed up than slow down

3. **AI Video Tools:**
   - **Descript** - Edit video like a document, AI voiceover
   - **CapCut** - Free, good for combining clips
   - **Canva** - Simple video from clips + text
